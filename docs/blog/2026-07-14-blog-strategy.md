# Blog Strategy: lelaboratoire.ma

## Executive Summary
lelaboratoire.ma sells lab equipment, chemical products, and small tooling (glassware, plastics) to labs, schools, hospitals, and industry in Morocco. Blog goal: dual SEO traffic (French/Darija-market search) + authority/trust with B2B buyers who research before requesting a quote. Cadence: 1 post/day.

## Market Context (Morocco lab-equipment suppliers)
Direct competitors found via search: equipement-labo.com, estlab.ma, etalonnagemaroc.com, assistec.ma, equipement-laboratoire.com, professional-labo.com, razilab.ma, universlabo.com, isolabmaroc.ma. None of these run active blogs with structured content — real content gap.

## Audience Segments

### Segment 1: Lab Procurement Manager (Decision stage)
- Role: buys equipment for university/hospital/industrial lab in Morocco
- Pain points: comparing specs, budget justification, import/delivery lead time, calibration/compliance
- Search behavior: "meilleur [équipement] laboratoire prix Maroc", "devis matériel labo"
- AI behavior: asks ChatGPT for spec comparisons, "best autoclave for small lab"
- Content pref: comparisons, buying guides, spec tables
- Buying stage: Consideration → Decision

### Segment 2: Lab Technician / Researcher (Awareness stage)
- Role: hands-on user, influences purchase but doesn't sign
- Pain points: correct usage, safety, maintenance, consumable sourcing
- Search behavior: "comment utiliser [équipement]", "verrerie laboratoire types"
- Content pref: how-to guides, safety content, technique explainers
- Buying stage: Awareness

### Segment 3: School/University Lab Admin (Awareness → Consideration)
- Role: equips teaching labs, budget-constrained
- Pain points: bulk pricing, safety compliance for students, durable/cheap tooling
- Search behavior: "équipement laboratoire scolaire Maroc", "matériel chimie collège"
- Content pref: listicles, budget guides, safety checklists

## Competitive Positioning
No Moroccan lab-equipment competitor runs structured blog content (checked: 7 sites, none with active blog/content hub). Differentiation: be first mover with real buying-guide + safety content in French for the Moroccan market — direct authority gap, low competition for AI citation on French-language lab-equipment queries.

## AI Citation Surface Strategy
On-site: answer-first H2s, citation capsules, 60-70% question-form H2s, FAQ schema every post, consistent French terminology (no synonym drift on key terms like "verrerie", "produits chimiques").

Off-site (highest leverage, 88-92% of AI citations are off-site):
| Channel | Action |
|---|---|
| YouTube | Product demo / how-to videos in French for pillar posts |
| Reddit/forums | Participate in Moroccan science-education or industry forums |
| Review platforms | Maintain profile visibility where B2B buyers check suppliers |
| Industry publications | Guest content in Moroccan industrial/education press |

## Content Quality Standards
- Blog score target 80+ (`/blog analyze`)
- 8+ tier 1-3 sources per post (safety data, standards bodies, manufacturer specs)
- 2+ charts, 3+ images per post
- 5+ internal links per post
- Article + FAQ schema on every post
- 1,500+ words spokes, 3,000+ pillars

## Content Velocity
- 1 post/day (per stated capacity) — front-load pillar pages first, then spokes
- Freshness updates: revisit pricing/regulatory posts monthly
